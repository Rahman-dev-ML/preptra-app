## Section 1: Sindhi Language Origins and Foundations

1. Sindhi Language Origins and Foundations (Easy)
1. Sindhi language belongs to which language family?
A) Dravidian
B) Indo-Aryan
C) Semitic
D) Turkic
Answer: B

2. The current Sindhi script is written in:
A) Devanagari
B) Arabic-Persian
C) Roman
D) Gurmukhi
Answer: B

3. Who wrote the book "Sindhi Boli"?
A) Dr. Nabi Bakhsh Baloch
B) Muhammad Ibrahim Joyo
C) Siraj
D) Mirza Qaleech Beg
Answer: A

4. The Sindhi Language Authority was established to:
A) Translate books
B) Promote and develop Sindhi language
C) Teach English
D) Write poetry
Answer: B

5. According to historians, the Holy Quran was first translated into Sindhi in:
A) 5th century
B) 8th or 9th century
C) 12th century
D) 15th century
Answer: B

6. Institute of Sindhology is primarily concerned with:
A) Teaching English
B) Studying Sindhi culture and language
C) Medical research
D) Agricultural studies
Answer: B

7. The modern standardized Sindhi script was finalized during the rule of:
A) Mughals
B) British
C) Kalhoras
D) Arabs
Answer: B

8. Sindhi Adabi Board functions to:
A) Publish Sindhi literature
B) Teach mathematics
C) Conduct exams
D) Build schools
Answer: A

9. Which scholar's theory suggests "Sindhi language springs from nature"?
A) Dr. Allana
B) Dr. Baloch
C) Various scholars debate this
D) Mirza Qaleech Beg
Answer: C

10. The study of Sindhi language from linguistic perspective is called:
A) Sindhiology
B) Linguistics
C) Etymology
D) Phonetics
Answer: A
