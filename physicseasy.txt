## Section 1: Vector Analysis and Operations

1. Vector Analysis and Operations (Easy)
1. Which of the following is a scalar quantity?
A) Force
B) Velocity
C) Temperature
D) Acceleration
Answer: C

2. The dot product of two perpendicular vectors is:
A) Maximum
B) Zero
C) One
D) Negative
Answer: B

3. A vector has magnitude 5 units. If it is multiplied by 3, its new magnitude is:
A) 5 units
B) 8 units
C) 15 units
D) 25 units
Answer: C

4. The cross product of two parallel vectors is:
A) Maximum
B) Zero
C) One
D) Infinity
Answer: B

5. Which operation on vectors results in a scalar?
A) Cross product
B) Addition
C) Dot product
D) Subtraction
Answer: C

6. A unit vector has magnitude:
A) Zero
B) One
C) Infinity
D) Variable
Answer: B

7. The gradient of a scalar field is a:
A) Scalar
B) Vector
C) Tensor
D) Constant
Answer: B

8. Vector addition follows which law?
A) Distributive law
B) Commutative law
C) Both A and B
D) Neither A nor B
Answer: C

9. The magnitude of vector A = 3i + 4j is:
A) 3
B) 4
C) 5
D) 7
Answer: C

10. The divergence of a vector field results in a:
A) Vector
B) Scalar
C) Matrix
D) Tensor
Answer: B
