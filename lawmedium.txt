## Section 1: Civil Procedure Code (CPC)

1. Civil Procedure Code (Medium)
1. The difference between actual and constructive res judicata is:
A) Actual is for same issue, constructive for related issues
B) Actual is for criminal, constructive for civil
C) No difference exists
D) Actual is temporary, constructive permanent
Answer: A

2. Res judicata applies when all these conditions are met EXCEPT:
A) Same parties
B) Same subject matter
C) Different court of competent jurisdiction
D) Matter directly and substantially in issue
Answer: C

3. A foreign judgment is NOT conclusive when:
A) Properly authenticated
B) Given by competent court
C) Obtained by fraud
D) On merits of case
Answer: C

4. Mesne profits can be claimed:
A) Only in original suit
B) In execution proceedings
C) Both A and B
D) Neither A nor B
Answer: C

5. Order 39 Rules 1 and 2 deal with:
A) Permanent injunction
B) Temporary injunction
C) Mandatory injunction
D) Perpetual injunction
Answer: B

6. A plaint can be rejected under Order 7 Rule 11 when:
A) It discloses a cause of action
B) Relief claimed is undervalued
C) Properly stamped
D) Within limitation
Answer: B

7. The doctrine of restitution applies when:
A) Decree is upheld
B) Decree is reversed
C) Suit is filed
D) Appeal is pending
Answer: B

8. Inherent powers under Section 151 CPC can be exercised:
A) To create new rights
B) To prevent abuse of court process
C) Against express provisions
D) Without jurisdiction
Answer: B

9. Representative suits under Order 1 Rule 8 require:
A) Different interests
B) Same interest in subject matter
C) Conflicting claims
D) Individual permissions
Answer: B

10. Additional evidence in appeal can be admitted when:
A) Appellant desires
B) Court requires for pronouncing judgment

Answer: B
