**Preparation Insights**

- **Translation & Composition:**\
  Work extensively on translating literary and technical texts between
  Arabic and English. Focus on preserving poetic imagery and formal
  language.

- **Literary Criticism:**\
  Develop critical essays on major historical periods (e.g., Jahili,
  Abbasid, Andalusian) and the contributions of key figures
  (al-Mutanabbi, al-Farazdaq, etc.). Read sample critiques and model
  essays.

- **Grammar & Linguistic Analysis:**\
  Revise topics such as derivation (اشتقاق), the structure of nominal
  and verbal sentences, and verb conjugations. Practice with examples
  and written exercises.

- **Historical & Cultural Context:**\
  Familiarize yourself with the cultural and historical background of
  major eras (e.g., the Abbasid golden age) to support your essay
  responses.

- **MCQs:**\
  Memorize key facts about Persian and Arabic literary heritage, famous
  works, and literary figures to answer multiple‐choice questions
  accurately.
