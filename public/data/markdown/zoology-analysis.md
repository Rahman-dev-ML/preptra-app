### Key Takeaways

- **High-Frequency Topics**: Water vascular system (Echinoderms), heart
  evolution (Chordates), Protozoa reproduction, polymorphism
  (Coelenterata), and placental mammals.

- **Compulsory Questions**: Focus on taxonomy (e.g., coelom types,
  insect legs), basic physiology (urea formation), and iconic examples
  (Archaeopteryx, Echidna).

- **Paper II**: Genetics (meiosis, linkage) and evolutionary theories
  dominate. Environmental issues like pollution and the nitrogen cycle
  are recurring.

**Study Priority**: Master Section A (invertebrates) and Section B
(vertebrate systems, evolution). Practice MCQs on taxonomy and
physiology. For Paper II, focus on genetics, cell biology, and
environmental topics.
