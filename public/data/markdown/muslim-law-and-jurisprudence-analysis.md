# 2014-2018

##### **D. Criminal Law**

##### **E. Legal Capacity and Contracts**

1.  **Al-Ahliyyah** (Legal Capacity) (2014 Q7, 2016 Q4).

2.  **'Aqd** (Contract) validity and capacity (2017 Q4).

##### **F. Historical Jurisprudence**

1.  Codification by **Abu Hanifa** (2015 Q2).

2.  **Imam Shafi'i** vs. Hanafis (2014 Q5, 2017 Q6).

3.  **Pre-Islamic Arabian customs** (2018 Q2).

##### **G. Modern Applications**

1.  **Constitution of 1973** and Islamic alignment (2014 Q4).

2.  **Federal Shariat Court** rulings (2018 Q5).

3.  **Shura** and modern parliament (2018 Q7).

#### **2. Most Frequently Repeated Questions**

1.  **Ijma and Ijtihad**

#### **3. Yearly Trends**

1.  **2014**: Foundational concepts (sources of law, family law basics).

2.  **2015**: Historical codification (Abbasid era) and inheritance law.

3.  **2016**: Quran as a legal code, critique of criminal law, women's

5.  **2018**: Customary influences, modern legislation (MFLO 1961,
    Sharia Court rulings).

#### **4. High-Yield Topics for Preparation**

1.  **Ijma/Ijtihad** and their modern applications.

2.  **MFLO 1961** and its judicial interpretations.

3.  **Marriage and Divorce** (dower, stipulations, talaq forms).

4.  **Inheritance** (unborn children, homicide, bequests).

5.  **Criminal Law** distinctions (Hadd vs. Ta'zir).

6.  **Maqāṣid al-Sharī'ah** and scholar debates (Al-Ghazālī vs.
    Al-Shāṭibī).

**Note:** Short notes on terms like *Talaq-i-Ahsan*, *Mushā*, *Sadqah*,
and *Dar al-Harb* appear frequently.
