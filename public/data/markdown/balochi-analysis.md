**1. Language, Culture & National Responsibility**

**Theme Description**

These questions focus on the role of language in national culture and
responsibility. They ask candidates to comment on the importance of
protecting and promoting the language, its cultural significance, and
the duties of society in this regard.

includes analysis of language style, poetic techniques, and the
technical presentation of literary works.

**Preparation Insights**

- **Translation Skills:**\
  Practice translating both technical and literary texts between English
  and Balochi. Emphasize idiomatic expressions and cultural nuance.

- **Critical Essay Writing:**\
  Develop structured outlines for essays on literary criticism and
  cultural narratives. Compare classical texts with modern trends and
  support your arguments with examples.

- **Debate & Identity:**\
  Read widely on Balochi language history and cultural identity debates.
  Be prepared to articulate your position regarding the origins and
  social role of Balochi language and literature.

- **Proverbs:**\
  Build a glossary of common Balochi proverbs and practice their
  translation to and from English.
