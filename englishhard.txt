## Section 1: Advanced Grammar and Syntax

1. Complex Grammar Structures (Hard)
1. In "It is essential that he be present," the verb "be" is in:
A) Present indicative
B) Present subjunctive
C) Past subjunctive
D) Infinitive mood
Answer: B

2. The sentence "Never have I seen such beauty" demonstrates:
A) Negative concord
B) Syntactic inversion after negative adverb
C) Passive transformation
D) Cleft construction
Answer: B

3. "The man whose son became a doctor" contains a:
A) Restrictive relative clause with genitive marker
B) Non-restrictive clause
C) Noun clause
D) Adverbial clause
Answer: A

4. In "The more carefully you work, the fewer mistakes you make," the construction is:
A) Comparative correlation
B) Double comparative
C) Parallel structure
D) Proportional comparative
Answer: D

5. "Having been warned of the danger, he proceeded cautiously" contains:
A) Present participle
B) Perfect passive participle
C) Gerund phrase
D) Infinitive phrase
Answer: B

6. The grammatical function of "that" in "The fact that he lied disturbs me" is:
A) Relative pronoun
B) Demonstrative adjective
C) Complementizer
D) Subordinating conjunction
Answer: C

7. "Were I to know the answer, I would tell you" uses:
A) Past subjunctive
B) Conditional inversion
C) Past perfect
D) Modal auxiliary
Answer: B

8. The construction "It was John who broke the vase" is called:
A) Extraposition
B) Cleft sentence
C) Pseudo-cleft
D) Topicalization
Answer: B

9. In "The meeting over, we left the room," the absolute construction indicates:
A) Simultaneous action
B) Causal relationship
C) Temporal sequence
D) Conditional relationship
Answer: C

10. "So devastating was the storm that thousands fled" exhibits:
A) Result clause with fronted intensifier
B) Causal adverbial
C) Concessive clause
D) Purpose clause
Answer: A
