## Section 1: Advanced Microeconomic Theory

B) Price rigidity in oligopolistic markets

A) Extra income
B) Difference between willingness to pay and actual price

D) Government revenue
Answer: B

A) Always reinforces substitution effect
B) May work opposite to substitution effect for inferior goods
C) Is always positive
D) Doesn't exist
Answer: B

7. Cross-price elasticity of complementary goods is:
A) Positive
B) Negative
C) Zero
D) Infinite
Answer: B

8. A firm should shut down in short run when:
A) Price falls below average total cost
B) Price falls below average variable cost
C) There are losses
D) Revenue declines
Answer: B

A) Wages are low
B) Income effect dominates substitution effect at high wages

A) Cooperation is always optimal
