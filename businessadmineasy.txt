## Section 1: Management Fundamentals

1. Management Fundamentals (Easy)
1. Management is best defined as:
A) Doing work yourself
B) Getting work done through others
C) Avoiding work
D) Only planning
Answer: B

A) Planning, Organizing, Leading, Controlling
B) Hiring, Firing, Training, Paying

Answer: B

Answer: B

B) Frederick Taylor

B) Esteem needs

A) Achievement
B) Recognition
C) Salary
D) Growth
Answer: C

8. In Herzberg's theory, motivators include:
A) Company policy
B) Working conditions
C) Achievement
D) Supervision
Answer: C

9. Decentralization means:
A) All decisions at top
B) Delegation of authority to lower levels
C) No decision making
D) Central location only

A) Physical reach
