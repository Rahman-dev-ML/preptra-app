## Section 1: Basic Earth Facts and Physical Geography

1. Basic Earth Facts and Physical Geography (Easy)
1. Approximately what percentage of Earth's surface is covered by water?
A) 51%
B) 61%
C) 71%
D) 81%
Answer: C

2. The Earth's core is primarily composed of:
A) Silicon and oxygen
B) Iron and nickel
C) Carbon and hydrogen
D) Aluminum and magnesium
Answer: B

3. The lithosphere refers to:
A) The atmosphere
B) The Earth's crust and upper mantle
C) The ocean floor only
D) The inner core
Answer: B

4. Which layer of the Earth is in a liquid state?
A) Inner core
B) Outer core
C) Mantle
D) Crust
Answer: B

5. The asthenosphere is characterized by:
A) Solid rock
B) Liquid metal
C) Partially molten rock
D) Gas
Answer: C

6. Who is known as the "Father of Geography"?
A) Aristotle
B) Eratosthenes
C) Ptolemy
D) Strabo
Answer: B

7. The study of earthquakes is called:
A) Volcanology
B) Seismology
C) Geology
D) Meteorology
Answer: B

8. The Richter scale measures:
A) Wind speed
B) Temperature
C) Earthquake magnitude
D) Ocean depth
Answer: C

9. The point on Earth's surface directly above an earthquake focus is called:
A) Hypocenter
B) Epicenter
C) Core
D) Fault
Answer: B

10. Continental drift theory was proposed by:
A) Harry Hess
B) Alfred Wegener
C) Charles Darwin
D) James Hutton
Answer: B
